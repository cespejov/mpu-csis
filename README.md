# Child Services Information System (CSIS)
### Social Impact Data Management for Underserved Communities

**Project Vision:** To provide non-profit organizations with a rigorous analytical tool for monitoring and improving the welfare of children in poverty-stricken regions through structured data tracking and progress analysis.

---

## 📝 Description
This application is a full-stack web portal designed to manage comprehensive information for children in need. By centralizing data on health, education, and household conditions, the system allows organizations to transition from anecdotal observation to data-driven support strategies.

The system was architected to support digitalization initiatives in regions where tracking long-term development in underserved populations is critical.

## 🚀 Core Features
*   **Comprehensive Child Profiles:** Track biographical data, specific poverty markers, and unique identification details.
*   **Education Tracking:** Monitor school enrollment, grade levels, attendance patterns, and academic achievements.
*   **Health & Nutrition:** Record medical history, vaccination records, nutritional status, and growth metrics to identify health risks early.
*   **Socio-Economic Mapping:** Document household income, living conditions (housing material, water access), and overall family stability.
*   **Guardian Management:** Maintain detailed records of primary and secondary caregivers, including contact information and legal relationships.
*   **Analytical Progress Dashboard:** Generate summaries that highlight a child's development over time and pinpoint specific areas (e.g., falling grades or health decline) that require immediate intervention.

## 🛠 Technical Stack
*   **Frontend:** Vanilla JavaScript for high performance and low-overhead rendering.
*   **Backend:** PHP for robust server-side logic and API integration.
*   **Database:** Relational SQL (MySQL) to maintain complex relationships between children, guardians, and historical records.
